The ledger, in photographs

Every block below is a live deployment — unposed, unfiltered, shot by the crews themselves. New frontiers append here as they launch.

Frontline — Canopy

Reforestation &
Ecosystem Recovery

Bare canal banks and sun-baked schoolyards across Sheikhupura, replanted with native neem, sheesham, jamun, and amaltas — then hand-watered through the summers and re-walked until each tree can hold its ground alone.

Active on this front

  • Iqra Shahid Survival Audits
  • Danish Ali Nursery · Jandiala
  • Sana Ullah Watering Rota
  • Rabia Noor School Groves

Frontline — Engines

Micro-Enterprise &
Independent Assets

Fully outfitted mobile fruit carts — welded, stocked, licensed, and route-walked — handed over as working businesses, not aid. The owner sets the prices. The takings never touch our hands.

Active on this front

  • Usman Ghani Cart Assembly
  • Ayesha Malik Vendor Paperwork
  • Tayyab Hussain Route Planning

Frontline — Craft

Artisan Craft &
Digital Direct-Trade

Village workshops photographed, catalogued, and connected straight to buyers through the Bazaar — makers paid first at full global value, every dispatch walked by a human logistics desk a few kilometres from the kiln.

Active on this front

  • Mehak Zafar Dispatch Logistics
  • Farhan Aslam Sourcing Runs
  • Hania Yousaf Craft Photography
